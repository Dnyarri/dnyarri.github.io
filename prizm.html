POVRay mosaic: prisms

Solid prism mosaics

Programs, converting PNG image into POVRay object, consisting of multiple prisms colored after source image pixels.

p6zaika produces hexagonal prisms, packed into regular 63 plane partition - a honey comb.

Prisms are scaled so that their height is controlled by source image brightness, adding some relief (like rough heightfield).

p3zaika produces triangular prisms, packed into regular 36 plane partition.

Objects are textured, texture can be further edited in POVRay.

Scaling may be easily changed.


Proceed to POVmosaic at GitHub for downloads.
