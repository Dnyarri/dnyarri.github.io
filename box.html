POVRay mosaic: boxes

Solid cube mosaics

Programs, converting PNG image into POVRay object, consisting of multiple cubes colored after source image pixels.

Cubes are packed into C4 plane (square grid).

With b4zaikaS, cubes are scaled so that box height is controlled by source image brightness, adding some relief (like rough heightfield).

b4zaikaR-produced boxes are rotated around z-axis according to source image brightness (and randomly around x, y), thus providing some artistic effect.

Cubes are textured, texture can be further edited in POVRay.

Scaling and rotation may be easily changed.
