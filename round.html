POVRay mosaic: spheres and cylinders

Sphere and cylinder mosaics

POV Sphere Mosaic - origin of all POV mosaic collection, initially created to simulate real technology of constructing image-like ads from real plastic spheres.

Currently rewritten as s3zaika, it produces scene made out of spheres with triangle packing.

Spheres have random vertical offset (controlled by general settings, easy to edit) to break visual patter uniformity.

s4zaika is a variant producing square pattern.

Objects are textured, texture can be further edited in POVRay.

c3zaika is a crossbreed between sphere and cube mosaics - it produces cylinder pack with cylinder height controlled by source brightness.

Scaling may be easily changed.


Proceed to POVmosaic at GitHub for downloads.
